Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How does the blind matching process work?

A: The blind matching process uses a sophisticated algorithm to match two users based on shared interests, location, and other criteria. Each user receives only one match per session, and we take care to avoid repeatedly matching the same pair.

Q: What safeguards are in place for women using the app?

A: We take the safety of all users seriously, but we understand that women may face unique risks when it comes to online dating. That's why we offer profile verification and block/report options to give women greater control over their experience. We also encourage all users to report any inappropriate behavior or harassment.

Q: How much time do users have to chat before deciding to meet in person?

A: Users have a limited time period to chat and get to know each other before deciding whether to meet in person. We believe that this helps to create a sense of urgency and avoid the endless back-and-forth that can happen on other dating apps. However, we also encourage users to take their time and make sure they feel comfortable before agreeing to a meeting.

Q: How can I provide feedback on my blind date experience?

A: We value your feedback and use it to improve our matching process for future users. After your chat has ended, you'll have the opportunity to rate your experience and provide feedback on what worked well and what could be improved. We appreciate your input and take it seriously!

Q: Can I customize my profile to showcase my personality?

A: Absolutely! We encourage users to fill out their profiles with as much information as possible, including hobbies, interests, and favorite music/movies/books. You can also upload photos to show off your unique style and personality.

Q: Are there any age restrictions for using the app?

A: Yes, users must be 18 years of age or older to use the app. We take this requirement seriously and ask users to verify their age when creating an account.

Q: Can I use the app to make friends or find platonic connections?

A: While the app is primarily designed for dating, we understand that some users may be looking for friendship or other types of connections. You can indicate your preferences on your profile and the matching algorithm will take that into account.

Q: How much does the app cost to use?

A: The basic features of the app are free to use, but we also offer premium features for users who want to upgrade their experience. Prices vary depending on the feature and the duration of the subscription.

Q: Is my personal information safe and secure on the app?

A: Yes, we take data privacy and security very seriously. We use encryption and other security measures to protect your personal information, and we never share or sell your data to third parties. However, we do encourage users to take basic precautions when sharing personal information online.
